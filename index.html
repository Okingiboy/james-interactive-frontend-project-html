About Me

James O. Chukwu (PhD) is currently the International Technical Manager HPO global Inc and director in Pioneer-Alfa Petroleum Services (PPS). Dr Chukwu was the President and CEO of Shale Technology Nigeria Ltd and oil/gas consortium.

Dr Chukwu was an Executive director Del Sigma Oil and gas and stirred the acquisition of the K field marginal in Bayelsa. Dr Chukwu was the former head of department of Petroleum Engineering at Federal University of Technology, Owerri, Nigeria. An author of Practical Certificate Physics, a science physics textbook for secondary and early tertiary institution, Dr Chukwu Trained the SPDC newly recruited staff at Shell Intensive Training School Warri, Nigeria.

At University of Texas, Astin, Boot Camp Coding.

Dr Chukwu holds a Bachelors, Masters and PhD degrees all in Petroleum Engineering and a member of the Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), member of The Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE). He is the Pastor of RCCG Everlasting King Chapel, Houston, Texas.

Work

Dr. Chukwu is a Petroleum Engineer with specialty in Computer Modeling and Reservoir Simulation.

He has handled several reservoir engineering and Field Development Studies for black oil and gas condensate systems worldwide: including Colombia, Venezuela, Saudi Arabia, and Nigeria.

He is a Petrel Reservoir Engineering (ECLIPSE) simulation engineer. Dr. Chukwu has carried out reservoir simulation studies using the PETREL RE in conjunction with the ECLIPSE simulator in field development studies worldwide

Dr Chukwu has more than 25 years of experience in Petroleum Engineering and the oil and gas industry. He has stirred and managed the studies and successful implementation of oil recovery and fields development plans (FDP) aimed at increased oil production worldwide.

He Led team studies of water injection and pressure maintenance simulation studies in the Niger Delta Nigeria, and gas condensate recovery for PDVSA in Venezuela.
